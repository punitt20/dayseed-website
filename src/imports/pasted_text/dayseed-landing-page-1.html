Design a complete, production-quality responsive landing website for my mobile productivity app called "DaySeed." APP NAME: DaySeed TAGLINE: "Plant Today. Grow Tomorrow." Use a clean, highly readable supporting font for body copy and UI. The typography should feel similar to a premium lifestyle or mindfulness brand rather than a tech startup. ================================================== NAVIGATION ================================================== Create a minimal top navigation.
